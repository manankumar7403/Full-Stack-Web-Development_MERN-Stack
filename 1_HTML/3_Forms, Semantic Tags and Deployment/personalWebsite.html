  • Python


    Python is a general-purpose, dynamic, high-level, and interpreted programming language. It supports Object Oriented programming approach to develop applications. It is simple and easy to learn and provides lots of high-level data structures.
    Python is an easy-to-learn yet powerful and versatile scripting language, which makes it attractive for Application Development.

    It can be used to develop Android apps, server-side apps and much more.

    C++ is a general purpose, case-sensitive, free-form programming language that supports object-oriented, procedural and generic programming.
    C++ is a middle-level language, as it encapsulates both high and low level language features.

My Favourite Food Recipes

  • Recipe of Chole Bhature

    Ingredients for Chole

    • 1 cup dried white chickpeas (kabuli chana)
    • 2 medium-sized onions, finely chopped
    • 2 tomatoes, finely chopped
    • 2-3 green chilies, slit lengthwise
    • 1-inch piece of ginger, grated
    • 4-5 cloves of garlic, minced
    • 1/2 cup fresh coriander leaves, chopped
    • 2-3 tablespoons cooking oil
    • 1 teaspoon cumin seeds
    • 1 teaspoon coriander powder
    • 1/2 teaspoon turmeric powder
    • 1 teaspoon red chili powder
    • 1 teaspoon garam masala
    • 1 teaspoon amchur (dry mango powder)
    • Salt to taste
    • Water for boiling chickpeas

    Ingredients for Bhature
    • 2 cups all-purpose flour (maida)
    • 1/4 cup semolina (sooji)
    • 1/4 cup plain yogurt (curd)
    • 1/2 teaspoon baking soda
    • 1 teaspoon sugar
    • Salt to taste
    • Water for kneading
    • Oil for deep frying

    Instructions for making Chole
    1. Wash and soak the dried chickpeas in water overnight or for at least 8 hours. Drain the water.
    2. Pressure cook the soaked chickpeas with enough water and a pinch of salt until they are soft and cooked. It usually takes around 3-4 whistles on medium heat. Once done, set aside.
    3. Heat oil in a large pan or kadai. Add cumin seeds and let them splutter.
    4. Add finely chopped onions, green chilies, ginger, and garlic. Sauté until the onions turn golden brown.
    5. Add chopped tomatoes and cook until they turn mushy and the oil starts separating.
    6. Add all the dry spice powders: coriander powder, turmeric powder, red chili powder, garam masala, and amchur. Mix well and cook for a couple of minutes.
    7. Add the boiled chickpeas along with some of the water used for boiling. Adjust the consistency of the gravy as per your preference. Add salt to taste and simmer for 10-15 minutes on low heat.
    8. Garnish with chopped fresh coriander leaves. Your Chole is ready!

    Instructions for making Bhature
    1. In a mixing bowl, combine all-purpose flour, semolina, baking soda, sugar, salt, and yogurt.
    2. Gradually add water and knead the dough until it becomes soft and smooth. Apply a little oil to the dough and cover it. Let it rest for about 2 hours.
    3. After resting, divide the dough into small balls and roll them out into oval or round shapes, not too thick.
    4. Heat oil in a deep frying pan on medium-high heat. Once the oil is hot, gently slide the rolled-out dough into the hot oil. It should puff up and turn golden brown. Fry until both sides are evenly cooked.
    5. Remove the bhature from the oil and place them on a paper towel to remove excess oil.

    Serve the hot and spicy Chole with the crispy Bhature. You can also serve it with pickles, sliced onions, and a side of yogurt for a complete meal. Enjoy your homemade Chole Bhature!


  • Recipe of Red Sauce Pasta

    Ingredients for Pasta

    • 2 cups of pasta (penne, spaghetti, or any pasta of your choice)
    • Water for boiling
    • Salt to taste
    • 1 tablespoon olive oil (optional)

    Ingredients for Red Sauce
    • 2 tablespoons olive oil
    • 1 small onion, finely chopped
    • 3-4 cloves of garlic, minced
    • 1 can (14 ounces) of crushed tomatoes or 2 cups of fresh tomato puree
    • 1 teaspoon dried oregano
    • 1 teaspoon dried basil
    • 1/2 teaspoon red chili flakes (adjust to taste)
    • Salt and pepper to taste
    • 1 teaspoon sugar (optional, to balance the acidity of tomatoes)
    • Fresh basil leaves or parsley for garnish (optional)
    • Grated Parmesan cheese for topping (optional)

    Instructions for Cooking the Pasta
    1. Boil the Pasta:
      • Bring a large pot of water to a boil.
      • Add a pinch of salt and a tablespoon of olive oil (optional) to the boiling water.
      • Add the pasta to the boiling water and cook according to the package instructions or until al dente (usually around 8-12 minutes). Stir occasionally to prevent sticking.
      • Once cooked, drain the pasta and set it aside.

    Instructions for Making the Red Sauce
    1. Heat Olive Oil:
      • In a large skillet or pan, heat 2 tablespoons of olive oil over medium heat.
    2. Sauté Onions and Garlic:
      • Add finely chopped onions to the hot oil and sauté for 2-3 minutes until they become translucent.
    3. Add Garlic:
      • Add the minced garlic and sauté for another 30 seconds to 1 minute until it becomes fragrant. Be careful not to burn the garlic.
    4. Add Tomatoes:
      • Pour in the crushed tomatoes or fresh tomato puree and stir well.
    5. Season the Sauce:
      • Add dried oregano, dried basil, red chili flakes (adjust to your spice preference), salt, and pepper to the sauce. You can also add a teaspoon of sugar if the tomatoes are too acidic. This will help balance the flavors.
    6. Simmer:
      • Reduce the heat to low and let the sauce simmer for about 15-20 minutes, stirring occasionally. This allows the flavors to meld and the sauce to thicken.
    7. Combine with Pasta:
      • Once the sauce has thickened to your desired consistency, add the cooked pasta to the skillet.
    8. Mix and Serve:
      • Toss the pasta in the red sauce until it's well coated. Allow it to heat through for a few minutes.
    9. Garnish and Serve:
      • Garnish with fresh basil leaves or parsley if desired.
      • Optionally, top with grated Parmesan cheese.

    Your delicious homemade red sauce pasta is ready to serve. Enjoy your meal!
